## What is an AI Advocate?

An AI Advocate is a champion within their team or department who:
- Understands the Dreamcobots ecosystem and "AI for Everyone" principles.
- Guides colleagues through adopting and using AI bots responsibly.
- Gathers feedback from users and relays it to the AI Governance Team.
- Celebrates wins and shares lessons learned across the community.

## Core Training Modules

### Module 1 — Dreamcobots Fundamentals (1 hour)
- How the ecosystem works: bots, orchestrator, tiers.
- Registering and running your first bot with `BuddyOrchestrator`.
- Understanding the FREE / PRO / ENTERPRISE tier model.

### Module 2 — Responsible AI (45 minutes)
- Reading the AI Policy and understanding key prohibitions.
- How to identify and report policy violations.
- Data classification: what data can bots touch?

### Module 3 — Driving Adoption (1 hour)
- Running a team AI demo session.
- Collecting feedback using the Engagement Tracker.
- Identifying high-value use cases for your department.

### Module 4 — Measuring Impact (30 minutes)
- Reading the Adoption Dashboard.
- Defining success metrics for your team.
- Reporting wins and blockers to the AI Governance Team.
