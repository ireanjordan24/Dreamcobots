# AI Champion Leader Guidelines

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** 2026-05-12  
**Audience:** Team Leads, Managers, and Department Heads

---

## Why Leaders Matter in AI Adoption

Research consistently shows that top-down endorsement is the single strongest
predictor of successful AI adoption. Leaders who actively champion AI:
- Remove blockers for their teams.
- Model responsible AI use.
- Connect business strategy to AI capabilities.
- Accelerate ROI by prioritising the right use cases.

---

## Leader Commitments

As an AI Champion Leader you commit to:

1. **Sponsoring your team's AI Advocate** — give them time and support.
2. **Including AI outcomes in team goals** — make adoption measurable.
3. **Attending one AI Governance review per quarter** — stay informed.
4. **Escalating policy concerns promptly** — protect your team and customers.
5. **Celebrating AI wins** — recognition drives more adoption.

---

## How to Champion AI in Your Team

### 1. Start with a High-Value Use Case
Work with your AI Advocate to identify one repetitive, time-consuming workflow
that a bot could own (e.g., report generation, lead qualification, data
ingestion). A quick win builds confidence and momentum.

### 2. Run an AI Show-and-Tell
Host a 30-minute demo where your Advocate shows the team a live bot run.
Invite questions and capture concerns. Use the Engagement Tracker to record
attendance and feedback.

### 3. Set a Team AI Goal
Add one AI adoption metric to your team's quarterly goals:
- "Deploy two bots this quarter"
- "Automate X hours of manual work per month"
- "Achieve a bot uptime of 95%+"

### 4. Remove Blockers
Common blockers you can address:
- **Access** — ensure team members have the right tier and credentials.
- **Time** — allocate dedicated time for training and experimentation.
- **Trust** — reinforce that AI is a tool, not a replacement; humans stay in control.

### 5. Review the Adoption Dashboard Monthly
Use the [Adoption Dashboard](../Metrics/adoption_dashboard.md) to track your
team's progress and identify where additional support is needed.

## Escalation Path

| Concern | Escalate To |
|---|---|
| Policy violation | AI Governance Team (immediate) |
| Bot security incident | Security team (within 1 hour) |
| Adoption blockers | AI Enablement Lead |
| Budget / tier upgrades | Finance + AI Governance |
