# DreamCo AI for Everyone

This directory houses the **"AI for Everyone"** enablement framework for the DreamCobots
ecosystem, aligned with GitHub's AI enablement model. Its mission is to make AI
accessible, responsible, and impactful for every member of the DreamCo community —
from beginners to enterprise power users.

---

## Directory Structure

```
DreamCo_AI_For_Everyone/
├── Policies/               Governance and compliance policies
│   ├── AI_POLICY.md        Master AI usage policy
│   ├── approved_tools.md   Vetted AI tools and integrations
│   └── data_security.md    Data handling and security requirements
├── Advocates/              Internal champion programme
│   ├── training_materials.md  Advocate onboarding & training
│   └── leader_guidelines.md   How leaders champion AI adoption
├── Learning/               Structured learning paths
│   ├── beginner_to_expert_path.md  Step-by-step learning journey
│   └── deployment_guide.md         Deploying bots to production
└── Metrics/                Adoption measurement
    ├── adoption_dashboard.md  How to read the adoption dashboard
    └── engagement_tracker.md  Engagement KPIs and tracking setup
```

---

## Five Pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|---|---|
| **Policies & Guardrails** | Responsible-use policies, approved tools, and data security rules |
| **Advocates Programme** | Training champion leaders and internal AI advocates |
| **Learning & Development** | Structured paths from zero to deployment |
| **Data & Metrics** | MAU tracking, adoption dashboards, and maturity scores |
| **Community of Practice** | Sharing wins, lessons, and reusable patterns |
