# DreamCo Communities of Practice

## Overview
Communities of Practice (CoPs) are groups of people who share a passion for a domain
and learn together through regular interaction. DreamCo CoPs accelerate AI adoption
by creating spaces for knowledge sharing, problem solving, and innovation.

---

## Active Communities

### #dreamco-general-ai
**Purpose**: General AI discussion, announcements, and cross-team collaboration  
**Cadence**: Ongoing  
**Platform**: GitHub Discussions (main)  
**Topics**: AI news, tool announcements, policy updates, wins and learnings

### #dreamco-dev-bots
**Purpose**: Bot development, code reviews, architecture discussions  
**Cadence**: Weekly async + monthly live session  
**Topics**: New bot patterns, framework compliance, testing strategies, debugging

### #dreamco-money-systems
**Purpose**: Revenue bots, Stripe integrations, monetization strategies  
**Cadence**: Bi-weekly  
**Topics**: Revenue metrics, new monetization patterns, pricing strategies

### #dreamco-real-estate-ai
**Purpose**: Real estate bots and market analysis  
**Cadence**: Weekly  
**Topics**: Property analysis, deal finders, foreclosure bots, cashflow calculators

### #dreamco-trading-ai
**Purpose**: Stock trading and crypto bots  
**Cadence**: Daily market discussion + weekly strategy review  
**Topics**: Algorithm updates, market signals, risk management

### #dreamco-growth-hacks
**Purpose**: Marketing bots, lead generation, growth automation  
**Cadence**: Weekly  
**Topics**: Lead gen strategies, social media automation, content bots

### #dreamco-automation
**Purpose**: GitHub Actions, n8n, LangChain workflows  
**Cadence**: Weekly  
**Topics**: Workflow optimization, new automation patterns, CI/CD improvements

## How to Participate
1. Join [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/DreamCo-Technologies/Dreamcobots/discussions)
2. Introduce yourself in the General discussion
3. Find a community matching your interests
4. Share a workflow, ask a question, or offer help

---

## Starting a New Community
If you see a gap, propose a new CoP via GitHub Discussions with the tag `new-community`.
Minimum 3 founding members required.
