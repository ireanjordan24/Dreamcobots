# DreamCo AI Acceptable Use Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines how DreamCo team members and contributors may use AI tools within
DreamCo systems, workflows, and products.

## Approved Uses
- Automating repetitive tasks using approved DreamCo bots
- Generating code, content, and reports with human review
- Researching market trends and summarizing data
- Drafting communications and documentation
- Optimizing workflows and pipelines
- Bot development, testing, and deployment

## Prohibited Uses
- Inputting personally identifiable information (PII) into unapproved AI services
- Sharing confidential business data with public AI models
- Generating misleading, deceptive, or harmful content
- Using AI to circumvent security controls or access controls
- Automating decisions with legal, financial, or medical impact without human review
- Training third-party models on DreamCo proprietary data without authorization

## Human Review Requirements
All AI-generated outputs used in the following contexts **must** be reviewed by a
qualified human before publishing or acting upon:
- Customer-facing communications
- Financial transactions or recommendations
- Legal or compliance documents
- Code deployed to production environments
- Any output used to make hiring or compensation decisions

## Reporting Violations
Report suspected violations to the AI Operations Director or open an issue tagged
`ai-policy-violation` in the GitHub repository.

## Policy Owner
AI Operations Director — reviewed quarterly.
