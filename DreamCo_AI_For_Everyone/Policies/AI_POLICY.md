# DreamCo AI Usage Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Effective Date:** 2026-05-12  
**Owner:** DreamCo Technologies — AI Governance Team

---

## Purpose

This policy establishes the principles and boundaries for using AI tools and
DreamCobots within DreamCo Technologies. It ensures that AI is used safely,
ethically, and in compliance with applicable laws.

---

## Scope

This policy applies to all DreamCo employees, contractors, and partners who
build, deploy, or consume AI-powered bots and services in the Dreamcobots
ecosystem.

---

## Core Principles

1. **Human Oversight** — AI decisions that affect individuals must be reviewed
   by a human before action is taken.
2. **Transparency** — Users must know when they are interacting with an AI
   system.
3. **Fairness** — Bots must be evaluated for bias before production deployment.
4. **Privacy** — Personal data must be anonymized before use in model training.
5. **Security** — AI outputs must be validated before being surfaced to end
   users.
6. **Accountability** — Every bot must have a named owner and a documented
   purpose.

---

## Approved Use Cases

- Automating repetitive business tasks (data entry, report generation).
- Customer support augmentation (not replacement without consent).
- Lead generation and qualification.
- Financial analytics and forecasting (with human sign-off on recommendations).
- Content drafting (reviewed by a human before publishing).

---

## Prohibited Use Cases

- Generating misleading, deceptive, or defamatory content.
- Scraping personal data without consent or legal basis.
- Autonomous financial transactions above $500 without human approval.
- Biometric data processing without explicit consent.
- Any use that violates GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, or other applicable regulations.

---

## Compliance and Enforcement

Violations of this policy should be reported to the AI Governance Team. Bots
found in violation may be suspended pending review. Repeated violations may
result in permanent deregistration from the Dreamcobots catalog.

---

## Review Cycle

This policy is reviewed quarterly. Changes are tracked in version control and
communicated to all stakeholders.
