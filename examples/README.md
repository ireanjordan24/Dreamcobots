### Practical Examples: Dreamcobot Ecosystem

#### Referral Bot Use Cases
1. **User Referral and Earnings Tracking**:
   - **Scenario**: Alex refers three friends (Casey, Jordan, and Taylor) to the Dreamcobots ecosystem.
   - **Process**:
     - Alex shares an invite link generated by the Referral Bot.
     - Referral Bot records Alex as the parent referrer for Casey, Jordan, and Taylor.
     - Each time Casey, Jordan, or Taylor achieves a milestone or generates revenue, Alex earns a percentage share.
   - **Outcome**: Alex sees referral earnings in real time through a dedicated dashboard.

2. **Bot-to-Bot Interaction**:
   - **Scenario**: Referral Bot communicates with Hustle Bot to optimize engagement.
   - **Process**:
     - Referral Bot identifies Taylor, who is underperforming based on generated revenue.
     - Sends notification to Hustle Bot to trigger a motivational campaign targeting Taylor.
     - Hustle Bot delivers tailored resources (e.g., tutorials) to help Taylor improve.
   - **Outcome**: Improved performance and higher earnings for Alex and Taylor.
