AI Transition Bot

The AI Transition Bot is a self-service onboarding kit that walks any business through the three mandatory steps of an AI adoption — understanding where you are, upskilling your team, and connecting AI to your tools.

1 · Readiness Assessment

Evaluates your company across six dimensions: data infrastructure, talent & skills, process automation, leadership & strategy, technology stack, and change management. Returns an overall score, a readiness level (beginner / intermediate / advanced), and a prioritised list of improvement recommendations.

2 · Training Modules

Enrol individual employees in AI learning paths at the right level. Free tier provides 5 beginner modules; Pro unlocks 30 modules including intermediate content; Enterprise gives unlimited modules across all levels including advanced AI engineering tracks.

3 · Integration API Kit

Activate plug-and-play AI connectors for your existing platforms. Supported platforms: crm · erp · hr · analytics · communication · document_management · e_commerce · supply_chain · finance · customer_support. Each activated integration returns a live endpoint URL and API key stub ready to wire into your workflow automation platform (n8n, Zapier, Make.com, etc.).

Quick Start

AI Transition Bot

from bots.ai_transition_bot.bot import AITransitionBot
from tiers import Tier

bot = AITransitionBot(tier=Tier.PRO)

# 1. Run an assessment
report = bot.run_assessment({
    "name": "Acme Corp",
    "industry": "manufacturing",
    "size": "medium"
})
print(report["readiness_level"])   # e.g. "intermediate"

# 2. Enrol an employee
bot.enroll_training({
    "employee_name": "Jane Smith",
    "level": "intermediate",
    "topic": "AI for Supply Chain"
})

# 3. Activate an ERP integration
integration = bot.activate_integration({"platform": "erp"})
print(integration["endpoint_url"])
# https://api.dreamcobots.ai/integrations/erp

AI Consulting Bot

from bots.ai_consulting_bot.bot import AIConsultingBot
from tiers import Tier

bot = AIConsultingBot(tier=Tier.ENTERPRISE)

# Match an expert
expert = bot.match_expert({
    "company_name": "HealthFirst Inc",
    "industry": "healthcare",
    "size": "enterprise"
})

# Book a session
session = bot.book_session({
    "company_name": "HealthFirst Inc",
    "expert_id": expert["expert_id"],
    "topic": "AI Adoption Strategy — Year 1"
})

# Generate an AI adoption roadmap
roadmap = bot.generate_roadmap({
    "company_name": "HealthFirst Inc",
    "industry": "healthcare",
    "readiness_score": 52
})
print(f"Roadmap: {roadmap['total_phases']} phases, "
      f"~{roadmap['estimated_total_weeks']} weeks")
